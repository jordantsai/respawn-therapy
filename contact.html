Update on coronavirus (COVID-19)

If you are a current patient and have any cold or flu symptoms, you will be asked to cancel your appointment and can resume in 2 weeks or following a negative test result. Free testing for LA county can be found here:
https://covid19.lacounty.gov/testing/

You can still continue your care from home with our e-visit program. Call or email today to schedule your appointments!

COVID-19 SAFETY PRECAUTIONS

▪ Patients will be scheduled for 45min sessions on the hour to provide a 15min gap between every patient

▪ E-visits will continue to occur on non-physically present days

▪ All patients will receive a temperature check and must confirm that they do not have any of the CDC listed symptoms of COVID-19 infection

▪ All staff and patients MUST wear a mask at all times within the facility

▪ All surfaces and equipment will be sanitized each use

▪ All patients and staff must wash hands before and after their session

▪ 6ft separation will be enforced as much as reasonably possible

Physical Therapy

New patients

Your first visit will primarily be an initial evaluation to determine specific impairments and to establish goals. All appointments are approximately one hour in length. Please wear comfortable clothes that can adequately expose areas for examination. Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, prescription for physical therapy, and any copies of surgical reports or imaging (MRI, CT, X-Ray). You may arrive 10 minutes early to fill out forms or fill them out ahead of time by downloading the forms here. intake form pdf

Appointments

Use the form below to contact us and we’ll call you back to schedule an appointment. Please be as detailed as possible. Include your insurance provider, referring physician, and diagnosis if possible. To help us best serve your inquiry, we recommend that you first describe the issue you’re having before telling us what you want to achieve. You may also call us directly to make an appointment. Our general response time is one business day.
