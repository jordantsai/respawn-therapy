About

Respawn Therapy (formerly Gail Wehner Physical Therapy) is a clinic that focuses on what matters most: the patient. We pride ourselves in providing those in our care one-on-one treatment without using any aides or PT Assistants in order to give you the best possible treatment. We will help you return to the things you love doing with a combination of manual therapy, education on how your body functions, and graded therapeutic exercises to improve the way you move.
