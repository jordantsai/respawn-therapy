Update on coronavirus (COVID-19)

If you are a current patient and have any cold or flu symptoms, you will be asked to cancel your appointment and can resume in 2 weeks or following a negative test result. Free testing for LA county can be found here:
https://covid19.lacounty.gov/testing/

You can still continue your care from home with our e-visit program. Call or email today to schedule your appointments!

×

COVID-19 SAFETY PRECAUTIONS

▪ Patients will be scheduled for 45min sessions on the hour to provide a 15min gap between every patient

▪ E-visits will continue to occur on non-physically present days

▪ All patients will receive a temperature check and must confirm that they do not have any of the CDC listed symptoms of COVID-19 infection

▪ All staff and patients MUST wear a mask at all times within the facility

▪ All surfaces and equipment will be sanitized each use

▪ All patients and staff must wash hands before and after their session

▪ 6ft separation will be enforced as much as reasonably possible

Physical Therapists

Jordan Tsai, PT, DPT

Dr. Tsai is a US Army veteran who served as a combat medic in Iraq from 2007-2008. He then went on to graduate from California University State University, Long Beach with his Bachelors of Science in Kinesiology: Exercise Science before attending the University of Southern California for his Doctorate in Physical Therapy. Jordan has experience working with a variety of orthopedic pathologies and populations ranging from young athletes to older, post-surgical patients. He is also an APTA Credentialed Clinical Instructor and enjoys mentoring DPT students in the clinic. In his free time, he enjoys drawing, watching sports, playing video games, and traveling.

Jennifer is also the Clinic Director as well as the Clinical Internship Director at SMPT. She enjoys eating new foods, traveling with her husband, and watching sports.
