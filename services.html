    SERVICES

Choose your Service Style

  • 1. Consultation Call

    Schedule a call with one of our Esports PTs and get an idea of whether PT is the right solution for your problem. We can help determine whether PT is the right solution for you or will direct you to the proper medical professional if not.

  • 2. In Person Evaluation/Treat

    A skilled Esports PT will come to you (Greater Los Angeles area only) and perform a detailed evaluation. They will provide you an explanation of potential pathology/source of pain and a comprehensive treatment plan based on your impairments.

  • 3. In-Person Follow up Visit

    The same Esports PT that performed your Evaluation will reassess your status so far and progress your treatment plan as needed. This is important to ensure continued improvement.

  • 4. Online Evaluation

    A skilled Esports PT will schedule an Evaluation with you via webcam. They will perform an assessment of your current symptoms and provide a treatment plan based on impairments and likely pathology. A great option during COVID-19 quarantine or if you are not located in Los Angeles!
